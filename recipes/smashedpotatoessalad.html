Smashed Potatoes Salad

A simple and delicious potato salad recipe.

Ingredients:

  • 2 Ibs baby potatoes
  • 1 tbs olive oil salt and pepper to taste
  • 3/4 cup Greek yogurt
  • 1/2 cup mayo
  • 2 tsp Dijon mustard
  • 1/2 large lemon
  • 4 garlic clove
  • 1 tsp bell pepper powder
  • 1/4 cup fresh dill
  • 1/4 cup fresh parsley
  • 1 medium cucumber
  • 1/2 red onion
  • Celery
  • 1 shallot

Steps:

  1. Boil the potatoes.
  2. Smash the potatoes.
  3. Cook the potatoes in the oven for 40 minutes at 180ยบ.
  4. Add Greek Yogurt, mayo, mustard, salt, pepper, bell pepper powder and lemon juice in a bowl.
  5. Chop the dill, red onion, cucumber, shallot, parsley and celery.
  6. Mince the garlic cloves.
  7. Add everything in the bowl.
  8. Add the smashed potatoes.
  9. Mix all the ingredients.
  10. Eat!
